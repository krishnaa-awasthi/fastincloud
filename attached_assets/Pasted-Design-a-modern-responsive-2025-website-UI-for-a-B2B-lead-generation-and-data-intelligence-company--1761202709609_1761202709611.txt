Design a modern, responsive 2025 website UI for a B2B lead-generation and data intelligence company named MQL Experts.

The site will be built using the MERN stack (React + Express + MongoDB), so the design should be component-based and ready for React implementation (clear component boundaries, reusable sections, modular layouts).

🎯 Design Goals

Build trust, look credible, and drive demo conversions.

Reflect a tech-forward, data-driven, and professional identity.

Follow modern B2B SaaS design standards (2025) — similar to ZoomInfo, Apollo.io, and Lusha.

🎨 Visual Style

Color palette: White/light-gray base with deep blue (#0061FF) and violet (#6C63FF) accents.

Typography: Inter or Poppins — bold, geometric, and clean.

Use large headings, generous spacing, rounded cards, and subtle gradients.

Visual tone: Futuristic, minimal, high-trust, business-class aesthetic.

Avoid stock photos — prefer vector or 3D illustrations related to analytics, data flow, or connections.

🧱 Page Structure

1. Navbar (Sticky)

Left: Logo

Center: Navigation links — Home | Solutions | Industries | Resources | About | Contact

Right: CTA button — “Book a Demo”

2. Hero Section

Headline: “Prospect Smarter. Connect Faster. Close Bigger.”

Subtext: “Empowering B2B marketing and sales teams with intelligent data that converts leads into customers.”

Buttons: “Get a Demo” (primary) + “Explore Solutions” (outline)

Background: abstract data-network pattern or 3D visualization

3. Trusted By Section

Row of 5–6 client logos with subtle grayscale styling

Caption: “Trusted by leading IT and SaaS companies worldwide.”

4. Solutions Section

Four cards with minimal icons and text:

Lead Generation

Sales Intelligence

Customer Profiling

Database Management

Hover interactions for each (slight lift or glow).

5. Why Choose Us Section

Split layout (left visual, right text).

Headline: “Data that drives real decisions.”

Bullet points: verified sources, real-time accuracy, ROI-driven strategies, custom segmentation.

6. Case Studies / Insights Section

3–4 blog/case cards with category tags, image, and title.

CTA button: “View All Insights.”

7. Testimonials Section

Carousel of 2–3 testimonials with photo, name, and company.

Background accent or subtle gradient for contrast.

8. CTA Banner Section (Full Width)

Gradient background (blue → violet).

Text: “Ready to supercharge your B2B pipeline?”

Button: “Book a Free Demo.”

9. Footer (Grid Layout)

Columns: Company | Solutions | Resources | Contact | Newsletter Signup

Include social icons, © 2025 MQL Experts, and Privacy/Terms links.

📱 Responsiveness

Design responsive variants for desktop (1440px), tablet (768px), and mobile (375px).

Use mobile-first approach with collapsible menu and floating “Book a Demo” CTA button.

⚡ Microinteractions & UX

Scroll reveal animations for sections (fade-up or slide-in).

Hover states for buttons and cards.

Use gradient underlines for active navbar links.

Smooth transitions between sections.

🧩 Output

Generate a Homepage UI design (desktop + mobile).

Clearly label React component equivalents: Navbar, HeroSection, SolutionsGrid, Testimonials, Footer, etc.

Include consistent color tokens and typography styles for export.

The final result should look modern, data-centric, high-conversion, and clean, matching 2025 SaaS website standards — fully ready to implement in MERN stack.
